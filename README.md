## ❌ THE PROBLEM
**Evaluating RFPs Is Slow, Manual, and Error-Prone:**
* **Read Long RFPs:** Complex documents with technical, operational, and business requirements.
* **Search Internal Records:** Company knowledge scattered across files, profiles, and past proposals.
* **Verify Capabilities:** Manual effort to match experience against each requirement.
* **Decide Under Pressure:** Slow evaluation leads to missed or poorly assessed opportunities.

### ⚠️ WHY IT MATTERS (The Cost of Manual RFP Evaluation)
* **Time Lost:** Hours spent searching for evidence that should be instantly retrievable.
* **Opportunities Lost:** Slow decisions mean missed bidding windows.
* **Requirements Missed:** Critical criteria overlooked in lengthy RFP documents.

**Target Users:** Software Companies · Consulting Firms · Government Contractors · Enterprise Sales Teams

---

## 💡 OUR SOLUTION
**Introducing RFPAgent:**
An AI system that compares RFP requirements against your company's knowledge base — delivering a fast, evidence-backed **Fit / No-Fit** decision. 

From upload to decision — RFPAgent replaces hours of manual research with seconds of AI-powered analysis through the following pipeline:
1. **Upload RFP**
2. **Retrieve Embeddings**
3. **AI Analyzes Fit/No Fit**

---

## 🧠 KNOWLEDGE BASE
**Creating the Company's Memory:**
RFPAgent builds a searchable semantic knowledge base from your existing company documents — turning scattered files into structured, retrievable evidence:
* Previous Projects
* Certifications
* Company Profiles
* Past Proposals
* Internal Documents

---

## ⚙️ WORKFLOW
**How RFPAgent Works:**
A seamless pipeline from document upload to AI-powered qualification results — powered by **FastAPI**, **Docling**, **ChromaDB**, and **LLM-based analysis**.

### 🤖 AI MATCHING (AI-Powered Requirement Matching)
The AI identifies strengths and gaps — surfacing exact evidence for every matched requirement:

* **What the RFP Requires:** React · AWS · FastAPI · Technical Support
* **What the Company Delivers:** Previous React Project (`✓`) · AWS Deployment History (`✓`) · FastAPI Backend Experience (`✓`) · Technical Support Services (`✓`)
