## PRJ-02: Nonprofit Research Institute - Cloud ERP Modernization

Nonprofit research &amp; education institute · 2022 - 2023

| Client         | Nonprofit research &amp; education institute   |
|----------------|------------------------------------------------|
| Period         | 2022 - 2023                                    |
| Contract value | $2,100,000                                     |
| Meridian role  | Prime contractor                               |

## Summary

Replaced fragmented legacy finance, FP&amp;A;, and grants-administration systems with a unified cloud COTS ERP, including HR/payroll and travel/expense integrations.

## Scope Delivered

-  Cloud ERP selection and implementation (finance, procurement, grants/fund accounting).
-  Fund classification controls per 2 CFR 200 and FASB ASU 2018-08.
-  Integrations with payroll/HRIS, banking/ACH, and a fundraising platform.
-  RBAC, PII handling, and SOC 1 / SOC 2-aligned controls.

## Technologies

NetSuite / Sage Intacct, REST integrations, SSO, ACH/e-payments.

## Outcomes

-  Eliminated duplicate manual reconciliations across 5 legacy systems.
-  Monthly close shortened from ~15 days to 5 days.
